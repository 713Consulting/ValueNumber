Calculate the Value Number™ to decide whether to adopt a new method or make a new purchase.

VN compares time, effort, and cost between your current method and a new one, or between your current setup and a potential purchase. Get S (without cost) and W (with cost). If the number is greater than 1, the new way likely wins.

How VN Works

Without cost, VN computes S. With cost, VN computes W. Results greater than 1 suggest the new method is favorable.

Without Cost (S)

Inputs: Z (old time), Q (old effort), Y (train time for new), V (new effort).

Formula: S = Z / (Y + V)

Interpretation: S > 1 favors adopting the new method.

With Cost (W)

Inputs: Z, Q, Y, V, plus M (old cost of current method), T (one-time training or purchase cost for the new option).

Formula: W = (Z×M) / (Y×T + V)

Interpretation: W > 1 favors adopting the new method.
